Our Impact

We protect, support and nurture the UK's most vulnerable children. For every £1 we spend, approximately 92p goes on working with the children and young people who most need our help.

Read about our impact ​​​​​​

What we do

  • Helping you foster a child

    Fostering is a really important role that can get a child through a difficult time. Your love and support can make a huge difference to their life – and to yours. And you won't be alone: we will give you all the specialist support you need.
     

    Find out more

  • Protecting children

    We support children who have been abused and help them feel safe again. We provide the long-term support they need and help prevent further cases of abuse. 

    Find out more

  • Supporting young people

    We offer practical and emotional support to young people so they can enter adulthood with the confidence they need to achieve their full potential.

    Find out more
